About me

Alejandro Zuniga was born, and raised in Wenatchee, Washington as a first generation Mexican-American. He enrolled in the local community college where he obtained his direct tranfer AAS-DTA(Associates of Arts and Science Direct Transfer Agreement), and transfered to Eastern Washington University, and is a current student majoring in Visual Communication Design (VCD) with a minor in Technical Communications
